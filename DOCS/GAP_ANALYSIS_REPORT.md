## 3Ô∏è‚É£ ROADMAP TO "PROFESSIONAL" STATUS

To bridge the gap and compete with professional lab equipment sites, we recommend the following development phases:

### üü¢ Phase 1: The "Professional Polish" (Weeks 1-4)
*Goal: Enhance the current B2C experience to look and feel premium.*
1.  **Document Library:** Add a field to products for uploading/downloading PDF manuals and Safety Data Sheets (SDS).
2.  **Enhanced Product Page:** Add tabs for "Specifications", "Documents", and "Q&A".
3.  **Reorder Button:** Add a "Buy Again" button in the "My Orders" history.
4.  **Guest Checkout:** Allow purchasing without creating an account (optional but common).

### üü° Phase 2: The "B2B Core" (Months 2-3)
*Goal: Enable businesses and labs to buy effectively.*
1.  **Request for Quote (RFQ):** Instead of "Add to Cart", allow users to "Request Quote" for high-value items.
2.  **Purchase Orders:** Add "Purchase Order" as a payment method in checkout (requires uploading a PO document).
3.  **Bulk Order Form:** A simple page where users can enter SKU + Quantity for 10+ items at once.
4.  **Tax Exemption:** Allow users to upload tax exemption certificates in their profile.

### üî¥ Phase 3: The "Enterprise" (Months 4-6+)
*Goal: Compete with Thermo Fisher/VWR.*
1.  **Corporate Accounts:** Allow a "Manager" to invite "Purchasers" and set spending limits.
2.  **Approval Workflow:** If a Purchaser buys > $500, it pauses for Manager approval.
3.  **Punchout Integration:** (Advanced) Allow big universities to browse your site from inside their own software.
